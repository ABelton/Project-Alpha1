About Tavaris

Tavaris is a Flint‑bred storyteller with a voice shaped by discipline, resilience, and evolution. His sound blends Hip‑hop grit with R&B soul, carrying the weight of lived experience and the clarity of a man who rebuilt himself. With a cinematic tone and a craftsman’s precision, Tavaris turns real moments into timeless narratives — balancing smooth melodies, raw honesty, and grown‑man depth. His music isn’t just heard; it’s felt, lived, and remembered.

About the Album

Two Worlds One Soul Two Worlds One Soul is a 20‑track odyssey through duality — the heartbreaks, the discipline, the rebuilding, and the rebirth that shape a man’s identity. Written and produced entirely by Avery Tavaris, the album fuses Flint roots with cinematic storytelling, emotional truth, and intentional craftsmanship. Every detail — from the songwriting to the engineering to the visual world — was created with purpose. This is not a playlist. This is a fully independent body of work built from the ground up, reflecting years of growth, restraint, and evolution
